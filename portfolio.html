Where we´ve been

Atletfabriken has been the authority on fitness since 1965 dating back to the original Atletfabrikens in Venice, California. It was the place for serious fitness. Atletfabrikens quickly became known as “The Mecca of Bodybuilding.” In 1977, Atletfabrikens received international attention when it was featured in the movie Pumping Iron that starred Arnold Schwarzenegger and Lou Ferrigno. From that first gym in Venice, Atletfabrikens has become the largest co-ed gym chain in the world. Today, Atletfabrikens has expanded its fitness profile to offer all of the latest equipment and services including, group exercise, personal training, cardiovascular equipment, spinning, Pilates and yoga, while maintaining its core weight lifting tradition. With over 3 million members world wide and over 650 locations, Atletfabrikens continues to change lives by helping people achieve their individual potential. Atletfabrikens is the preferred gym to the entertainment industry, amateur and professional athletes, and anyone else who is serious about fitness.
